Sprinklr —

Last October, I joined Sprinklr's Experience Design team in Portland, Oregon. Ever since then, I have been working on two of their core products — Content Marketing & Display.

Content Marketing is a collaborative workspace that allows companies to create, plan, publish, and measure the impact of their content. For this product, I was responsible for rethinking and redesigning the Production Table flows (which is a table view of all tasks and/or campaigns that are ongoing).

Currently, I am redesigning some features on Display — a Data Visualization & Digital Signage Solution. Display allows brands to find and analyze their best social content.

For the past few months, I had the opportunity to work collaboratively with UX Designers, AVP of Product Design, and front-end engineers. I learned and refined my skills in product thinking and technical prototyping. I'd love to discuss more, so please let me know if you have any questions.
