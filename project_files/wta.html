We the Adventurers —

We the Adventurers is a brand and resource that aims to help prospective study abroad goers find the right destination to pursue their international education. It aims to publish crowd-sourced reviews, links to student blog posts, and videos about student experiences. We the Adventurers also has merchandises such as tags, stickers, and t-shirts for purchase.

Problems —

  1. Lack of efficient platform and resource for interested students to easily browse through returnees' "reviews" on experiences
  2. Difficulties reaching out to returnees

Solutions —

  1. A general review platform created specifically for studying abroad
  2. Easily search by countries of interest and able to filter through type of reviews (blog, video, or general reviews)
  3. Easily and quickly leave a review with a click of a button

Logos & Stickers Design —

When developing the logo and stickers for this brand, I focused on creating very simple illustrations that are attention catching and playful. I used an illustration of the globe as the main design to parallel the mission and theme of the brand. I left the stroke unfilled to create a clutter-free and simple look. The first three designs are logos (only stroke, filled, and with words) and the last two are stickers. I created three logo designs with small variations to fit different occasions--for example: letter head, website, etc.
